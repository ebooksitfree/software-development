Book Description
-----------------

Qualified SharePoint administrators are in demand, and what better way to show your expertise in this growing field than with Microsoft's new MCTS: Microsoft Office SharePoint Server 2007, Configuration certification. Inside, find everything you need to prepare for exam 70-630, including full coverage of exam topics-such as configuring content management, managing business intelligence, and more-as well as challenging review questions, real-world scenarios, practical exercises, and a CD with advanced testing software.
