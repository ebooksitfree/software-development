Book Description
-----------------

 Computer Fundamentals and Programming in C, with its abounding, extensive chapter-end questions and unique pedagogy, is structured to address the challenges faced by novices as well as amateur programmers. Assuming no prior knowledge of programming languages, the book presents the reader with a rich collection of solved examples and exercises.        Show and hide more
