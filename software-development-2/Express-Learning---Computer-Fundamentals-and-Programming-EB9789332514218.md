Book Description
-----------------

 Express Learning is a series of books designed as quick reference guides to important undergraduate and postgraduate computer courses. The organized and accessible format of these books allows students to learn important concepts in an easy-to-understand, question-and-answer format. These portable learning tools have been designed as one-stop references for students to understand and master the subjects by themselves.        Show and hide more
