Book Description
-----------------

 Neal Whitten presents a highly practical guide to software project management, using a model that builds on the strengths of functional projectized and matrix organizations, while reducing or eliminating their weaknesses. You will recognize proven and familiar ways to define key roles and responsibilities, while also discovering exciting new organizational ideas for software projects.   Throughout the book, Whitten shares lessons that have a profound impact on your ability to draw out project members and help them realize their full potential, while performing the same valuable function for yourself as project leader.
