Deliver a successful SharePoint solution to your organization
Take control of the complex requirements for delivering a SharePoint 2013 solution to your organization. Led by a SharePoint MVP specializing in SharePoint service delivery, youâ€™ll learn proven methods to help you prepare for a smooth adoption and governance process throughout the enterprise.
